    • Summary: Latitude vs Longitude

    The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equater. To accomplish this analysis, we got data from the OpenWeatherMapAPI to assemble a dataset on over 500 cities.
    Then, we looked at the relationship between humidity, temperature, windspeed and cloudiness between latitude and longitude.
    In conclusion, an equatorial climate is a type of tropical climate in which there is no dry season. It is usually found at latitudes within five degrees of the equator and tropical rainforest is the natural vegetation.
